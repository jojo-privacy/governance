# Code of Conduct

Jojo Privacy is committed to fostering a respectful, professional, and inclusive open-source community.

This Code of Conduct applies to all participants, including maintainers, contributors, and community members.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:
- Communicate respectfully and professionally
- Focus on technical merit, not personal opinions
- Welcome constructive criticism
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Act in good faith

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Dismissive or aggressive communication
- Intimidation or threats
- Trolling or deliberate disruption
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass security or review processes

---

## Security and Privacy Respect

Special expectations apply due to the nature of this project:
- Do not publicly disclose vulnerabilities
- Follow responsible disclosure practices
- Do not pressure maintainers to rush sensitive changes
- Treat security discussions seriously and discreetly

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Actions may include:
- Warning
- Temporary restriction from participation
- Permanent removal from the project

Severity and context will be considered.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:
- Report it privately to the maintainers
- Do not escalate publicly

All reports will be handled confidentially and respectfully.

---

## Commitment

By participating in this project, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct and contribute to a professional and trustworthy environment.
